Test Pattern is an iPhone app that helps you discover colors.




It's free and in the App Store. Have at it!



   

  • Follow @testpatternapp to get announcements and check out nifty patterns. Send any comments and questions that way, too!
  • Check out a demo.
  • If you find patterns or colors you want to share with others, send them to testpatternapp@gmail.com.
  • I'll post them to @testpatternapp, then we'll celebrate them! It'll be like a contest, except without any losing. Whew!
