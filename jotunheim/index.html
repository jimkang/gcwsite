Jotunheim!

Don't-repeat-yourself microblogging.





Write what you think.

Life is thoughts and events. Events are often shared with other people. Thoughts, however, just sit in your head until they expire. Why not share a bit of your inner life? If not with your friends, then with your future self?

Sometimes, the answer is that you don't have anything on hand to save them. Or, it's just too much effort to share them with the people to which you'd like them to go.

Well, how about this, friend: Jotunheim can be with you at all times, and your thoughts can go there. Just pull out your phone and write or photograph. And with a tap, they can go from Jotunheim to pretty much wherever you'd like!

Post everywhere you want.

Jotunheim can post your entries to:
  • Twitter
  • Facebook
  • Tumblr
  • Flickr
  • mlkshk

It can post to all of them, just one of them, or none of them. You don't have to set up any forwarding chains or do any copying and pasting. Just write, then tap.

Have it forever.

In addition to flying cars and space karate, what makes the future interesting is that you don't quite know what's going to be around.

The dominant social networks might not be around anymore, or maybe they will be but you'll have closed your accounts for whatever reason. But you'll still have your entries in Jotunheim, and you'll be able to easily browse and search them.

Or if you've moved on from Jotunheim, you'll have your entries a text file that you can take anywhere because Jotunheim lets you export your entries in an open format.

Other fun facts about Jotunheim

  • It supports portrait and landscape orientations for maximum typing comfort.
  • There's help for every view, in case you get lost. Just hold down two fingers.
  • Supports images and video in your entries.
  • It can post in the background, freeing you up to do other things while it works.
