Jotunheim!

A great app for expressing yourself no matter where you are.




Jotunheim demo on Vimeo.




Follow @jotunapp on Twitter!

Post everywhere.

Jotunheim can post your entries to:

  • Twitter
  • Facebook
  • Tumblr
  • Flickr
  • mlkshk

It can post to all of them at once, just one of them, or none of them.

You can keep all of your people up-to-date, even if they're not all on the same social network. Just write, then tap.

Write whenever.

Whenever you think of something, Jotunheim can hold it for you. Just pull out your iPhone and start writing or photographing.

Before you know it, you'll have a collections of the thoughts and events of your life, captured in writing, photos, and video.

Keep your writing forever.

In addition to flying cars and space karate, what makes the future interesting is that you don't quite know what's going to be around.

The dominant social networks might not be around anymore. Or perhaps you'll have quit some of them. But you'll still have your entries in Jotunheim, and you'll be able to easily browse and search them. You can quit without fear.

Or if you've moved on from Jotunheim, you'll have your entries in a text file that you can take anywhere because Jotunheim lets you export your entries in an open format.

What else?

  • It supports portrait and landscape orientations for maximum typing comfort.
  • There's help for every view, in case you get lost. Just hold down two fingers.
  • Supports images and video in your entries.
  • It can post in the background, freeing you up to do other things while it works.
