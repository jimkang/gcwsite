About Ghost Crab Workshop

Ghost Crab Workshop makes two kinds of software:

  • Software that makes lives easier.
  • Software that expresses an idea or feeling.

In both cases, I prioritize consistency and reliability, both in functionality and design.

Ghost Crab Workshop is run by me, Jim Kang. I like software, board games, podcasts, mixed martial arts, composing music, and carnitas. I live in Cambridge, MA with my wife and cat, who are both pretty cool.

In addition to Ghost Crab Workshop's apps, I've worked on the teams at Modo Labs that built the Harvard, Technology Review, and Harvard Reunions iOS apps, among others.

My work in the past few years has centered around Cocoa on iOS, Objective-C, REST-based web services, and Unity. But prior getting into iOS development, I worked in web development. Before that, I spent several years as a native Windows developer.
