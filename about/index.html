About

Ghost Crab Workshop makes two kinds of software:


  • Software that makes lives easier.
  • Software that expresses an idea or feeling.

In both cases, Ghost Crab Workshop prioritizes consistency and reliabity, both in functionality and appearance.


Ghost Crab Workshop consists of one person. My name is Jim Kang. I develop and design (and market, sort of) Ghost Crab Workshop's apps. I enjoy software, but I also enjoy board games, podcasts, mixed martial arts, composing music, and carnitas. I live in Cambridge, MA with my wife and cat, who are both pretty cool.


In addition to Ghost Crab Workshop's apps, I've contributed to the teams at Modo Labs (great company; builds mobile apps and services for large institutions) that were behind the Harvard, Technology Review, and Harvard Reunions iOS apps. Before getting into iOS development, I worked in web development, and before that, I spent several years as a native Windows developer.

Depending on when you ask me, I may be available for consulting on your iOS project.

Be excellent to each other.
